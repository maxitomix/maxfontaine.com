METHODOLOGY

Project Definition and Planning:

  • Scope of the Project: Define what is and isn't included in the scope. Define goals, and deliverables of the project.
  • Functionality and Features: define needs of client on their website, such as an online shop, a booking system, or interactive forms.
  • Hosting and Domain.
  • Timeline: Initial consultation and design to development, testing, and deployment.

Design and Content:

  • Content: Decide who will be responsible for creating and providing the content.
  • Target Audience & Competitor Analysis.
  • Branding Guidelines.

Management:

  • Maintenance and Updates: Who will handle updates and how often they will be done.
  • Feedback and Approval Process.
  • Point of Contact. This should be someone who can make key decisions and provide prompt feedback.
  • Cancellation Policy.
